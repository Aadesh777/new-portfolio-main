Building aadeshdahal.com.np from scratch

My main portfolio was built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks. The goal was to learn exactly how SEO and GEO mechanics work under the hood: JSON-LD Person schema, Open Graph tags, sitemap structure, and semantic HTML. The hardest part wasn't the code — it was deciding what information actually helps Google and AI search engines understand who I am, without stuffing keywords unnaturally.
