About me

From design tools to search engines

A short timeline of how I got here — and what each stage taught me.

Early interest

Design first

I started exploring the digital world through design — Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva were my first tools. I enjoyed making things look right before I understood why they worked.

The turning point

Getting curious about "why"

At some point I started wondering why certain pages show up first on Google and others don't, even when they look just as good. That question is what pulled me toward SEO.

Current chapter

BCA, 7th semester

I'm currently studying at Madan Bhandari Memorial College, in my 7th semester of BCA, while learning SEO fundamentals — on-page optimisation, keyword research, technical SEO, and structured data — alongside my design background.

Right now

Building in public

My main portfolio and this site are both part of one live experiment: can a student from Jhapa build a real, search-visible personal brand using nothing but HTML, CSS, JS, and consistent effort? This site documents that process honestly.

Where I'm from

Jhapa, a district in the eastern terai of Nepal. It's part of how I introduce myself, online and offline.

What I'm studying

Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA), 7th semester, at Madan Bhandari Memorial College.
